About Us

QuizInc is the official qizzing club of NIT Durgapur. QuizInc was started by two students Harish Mohan and Anup Nair in 2003 and is now run by a group of quizzing enthusiasts from the college. With every passing year we have reached new heights and we strive to take it even higher.

We not only organize sessional quizzes but also Quizzitch Cup, a fest dedicated exclusively to quizzing. We release an annual maqazine called QuizInk.

Quizzitch Cup 2019

We are back with the biggest quizzing fest of the college, Quizzith Cup 2019. It will take place 1st to 3rd February. Display your knowledge and bag some exciting prizes. Posters and details will be uploaded soon. Stay tuned.
